Guns N Roses

Full Details

Guns N’ Roses is an American hard rock band formed in 1985 in Los Angeles. Known for their raw energy, powerful vocals, and iconic guitar riffs, they became one of the biggest rock bands in the world during the late 1980s and early 1990s. Their debut album Appetite for Destruction became a massive success and is one of the best-selling debut albums of all time. Their biggest breakthrough hit: Sweet Child o' Mine – This song became their only #1 single on the Billboard Hot 100 and made them globally famous. Other iconic tracks: Welcome to the Jungle and November Rain Over 100 million records sold worldwide Appetite for Destruction is one of the best-selling albums in history Inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame in 2012 Known as one of the greatest hard rock bands of all time
